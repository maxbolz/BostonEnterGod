About Me
At the time of this site's creation, I am a first-year student at Northeastern University. This website is a project for INSH 2102 - Bostonography.
About This Project
This project explores the disappearance of churches in Boston between 1902 and 2023 using Bromley atlases, Google Maps, and demographic data. NOTE: some pages of the atlases were not used, as the later ones do not document specific types of buildings.
About The Process
This project was heavily aided by the use of Brandfolder OCR. With decent accuracy, I was able to identify the names of churches in each atlas plate. From there, it was a matter of searching the images.
About The 1902 Maps
The 1902 maps on the front of the cards on the main page are courtesy of Bromley atlases, which created fire atlases in the 1900s. The church highlighting was done by me in Photoshop.
About The 2023 Maps
The 2023 maps on the back of the cards on the main page are courtesy of Google Maps. Again, some edits are made by me in Photoshop.
